About Tabatha

Hi, I’m Tabatha.

I’m the owner, DJ, and MC behind Cherie Entertainment, and music has been a big part of my life for as long as I can remember. Getting into DJing wasn’t just about the love of music. It is about the positivity that flows through every event and the love the group shares. That energy is what drives me and I bring it to every single event I take on.

What makes me different? I read the room. Whether your group wants high-energy anthems, smooth background music, or an interactive karaoke experience, I adapt in real time to keep the energy exactly where it needs to be. No two events are the same, and every set I curate is built from scratch just for you.

I specialize in weddings, corporate events, private parties, and karaoke across the greater Phoenix area. When you book Cherie Entertainment, you’re not just hiring a DJ. You’re getting a partner who’s fully invested in making your event extraordinary.

“Kind, reliable, and always going above and beyond to ensure the celebration is unforgettable.”
Owner · DJ · MC · Phoenix, AZ

Meet the Team

Cara

What started as always being the friend who picked the playlist or nudged people toward the mic eventually led me to step behind it myself, and I have been hooked ever since.

I have been part of the Cherie Entertainment team for 4 years and I love being part of a team that genuinely cares about the details and the experience, not just the performance.

My strength is analyzing the crowd. Whether I am working with a confident performer or coaxing a first-timer to the mic, I make everyone feel comfortable, included, and part of the show.
