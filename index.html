About Tabatha

Hi, I'm Tabatha.

I'm the owner, DJ, and MC behind Cherie Entertainment — and I've been setting the tone for Phoenix's most memorable events since 2016. From the first song to the last dance, I bring energy, professionalism, and a genuine love for what I do to every event I take on.

What makes me different? I read the room. Whether your crowd wants high-energy anthems, smooth background music, or an interactive karaoke experience, I adapt in real time to keep the energy exactly where it needs to be. No two events are the same — and neither are my playlists.

I specialize in weddings, corporate events, private parties, and karaoke across the greater Phoenix area. When you book Cherie Entertainment, you're not just hiring a DJ — you're getting a partner who's fully invested in making your event extraordinary.
